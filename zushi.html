Project Overview

Problem

Sushi ordering apps often feel cluttered, making it hard for users to customize orders or complete checkouts smoothly.

Goal

To entirely reinvent the sushi ordering experience by creating an intuitive app that makes it simple for the users to browse, customize and order their ideal sushi plates.

Responsibilities

Conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and gathering feedback from peers and potential users to refine the app's interface and enhance the overall user experience.

User Research

I used surveys and interviews to gather information about the tastes and expectations of potential users in order to shape Zushi. At first, I thought that ordering quickly and with ease of navigation would be the main priorities. Nonetheless, user feedback indicated a complex need for an interface that is both aesthetically beautiful and efficient. Customers emphasized how important it is to customize their orders and showed a clear preference for personalization choices. The process of doing user research was essential in helping me refine my assumptions and match the functionality of the app to the wide range of needs of targeted users.

After a hectic day at her restaurant, Sophia Lee, an Executive Chef, looks forward to ordering sushi for dinner to explore new flavors. However, frustrated by the limited customization options online, she seeks alternatives to accommodate her family's preferences and dietary needs.

Goals

Frustrations

Takeaways

What I learned
I discovered the importance of prioritizing user needs and considering accessibility from the start of the design process.
Impact
The users praised the intuitive interface and smooth ordering experience, which boosted satisfaction and engagement levels significantly. One of my peers shared their thoughts, saying, "I had a great experience using the app—so easy to navigate and customize orders!"
Additionally, I would...
  • Refine the user interface based on user feedback to enhance the ordering experience.
  • Explore options to expand the app's features, such as integrating delivery options and offering more sushi recipes.
  • Conduct usability testing with a larger user group to validate improvements and gather further insights for iteration.
